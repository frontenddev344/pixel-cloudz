Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 6, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how PixelCloudz.com ("PixelCloudz", "we", "us") collects, uses, and protects information when you visit our website.

Information We Collect

We may collect information you voluntarily provide, such as your name and email address when subscribing to our newsletter or submitting a contact form. We also automatically collect standard technical data, including IP address, browser type, device information, and pages visited, via cookies and analytics tools.

How We Use Information

We use collected information to operate and improve the site, respond to inquiries, send newsletters (only to those who opt in), and serve relevant advertising. We do not sell personal information to third parties.

Cookies

PixelCloudz uses cookies for analytics, site functionality, and advertising personalization. You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings, though some site features may not function properly without them.

Advertising and Google AdSense

PixelCloudz may display advertising served by Google and other third-party advertising companies ("vendors"). Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to visitors based on their visit to this site and/or other sites on the internet. These vendors may use cookies, web beacons, and similar technologies to collect information about your visits to this and other websites in order to provide relevant advertisements.

You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings. You can also review the Network Advertising Initiative's opt-out page or download the Google Analytics opt-out browser add-on to prevent data from being used by Google Analytics.

Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services such as analytics providers and advertising networks that collect information under their own privacy policies. Some pages may contain affiliate links, for which we may earn a commission at no additional cost to you.

Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information, but no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete your personal data. To exercise these rights, contact us at contact@pixelcloudz.com.

Children's Privacy

PixelCloudz is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
